ABOUT SYL

StartupYourLife is an initiative lead by innovative
entrepreneurs to build a Startup Community in Morocco.

We believe a strong community is key to lay the foundation of a vibrant
startup ecosystem. We are passionate, a bit foolish but we are committed to
make it happen.

Our community counts one of the most successful entrepreneurs whom have a
link with Morocco, whether they are Moroccans living in Morocco or abroad
but also international entrepreneurs based in Morocco.

Mission

Our mission is to contribute in creating success
stories in Morocco that will inspire others and
attract local and foreign investors’ attention.

Our focus is on growing existing startups first.
As entrepreneurs need to be well surrounded,
our community welcomes passionate wannabes,
designers, hackers and brilliant minds whom
contribute to making our mission a success.

Vision

Our vision is to be the Hub of innovative
startups in Morocco and the link to other
ecosystems.

Our ambition is to rank Morocco’s startup scene
among the most dynamic ones in the MENA
region within the next 5-10 years.

WHAT WE DO

We identify and gather innovative entrepreneurs that have a link with
Morocco, whether they are Moroccans living in Morocco or abroad but also
international entrepreneurs based in Morocco. We help them share their
experience and knowledge, connect them with investors, mentors, experts and
PR and give them the visibility they need in both the local and international
ecosystem.
