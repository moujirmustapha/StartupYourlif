ABOUT SYL

StartupYourLife is an initiative lead by innovative entrepreneurs to build a Startup Community in Morocco.

We believe a strong community is key to lay the foundation of a vibrant startup ecosystem. We are passionate, a bit foolish but we are committed to make it happen.

Our community counts one of the most successful entrepreneurs whom have a link with Morocco, whether they are Moroccans living in Morocco or abroad but also international entrepreneurs based in Morocco.
